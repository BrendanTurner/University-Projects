First entering service in September 2077 with the U.S Army, the Fat Man is a shoulder mounted mini nuke launcher with a very powerfull payload. After initial experiments with earlier prototypes, the main hurdle that needed to be overcome was to find a way to fire the heavy ammunition far enough away from the man-portable system to keep the operator outside of the blast radius while also keeping the weight reasonably light. To do this there is a propellant charge within the miniture nuke ammnition itself rather then in the launcher similar to a standard bullet or shotgun cartridge.

Later models can be fitted with an alternate MIRV launcher which splits the intital projectile into six smaller randomly spread projectiles(Use With Caution!)
